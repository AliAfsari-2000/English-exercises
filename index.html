Active and Passive Voice Exercise

1. Am + past participle

1. Active: They invite me to the party.

Passive: I ___ to the party.

2. Active: They call me Ali.

Passive: I ___ Ali.

3. Active: They choose me for the team.

Passive: I ___ for the team.

2. Is + past participle

4. Active: They clean the room.

Passive: The room ___ every day.

5. Active: She writes a letter.

Passive: A letter ___ by her.

6. Active: They make a cake.

Passive: A cake ___ by them.

3. Are + past participle

7. Active: They wash cars.

Passive: Cars ___ every morning.

8. Active: They build houses.

Passive: Houses ___ by them.

9. Active: They paint the walls.

Passive: The walls ___ blue.

4. Was + past participle

10. Active: They repaired the car.

Passive: The car ___ yesterday.

11. Active: She wrote a book.

Passive: A book ___ by her.

12. Active: They built a bridge.

Passive: A bridge ___ last year.

5. Were + past participle

13. Active: They cleaned the rooms.

Passive: The rooms ___ yesterday.

14. Active: They painted the walls.

Passive: The walls ___ blue.

15. Active: They opened the doors.

Passive: The doors ___ at 8 o'clock.

6. Will be + past participle

16. Active: They will finish the work.

Passive: The work ___ tomorrow.

17. Active: She will write a report.

Passive: A report ___ by her.

18. Active: They will build a school.

Passive: A school ___ next year.
