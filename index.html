Active and Passive Voice Exercise

1. Present Simple (am / is / are)

1. Active: They clean the room.

Passive: The room ___ by them.

2. Active: She writes a letter.

Passive: A letter ___ by her.

3. Active: They make cars.

Passive: Cars ___ by them.

2. Past Simple (was / were)

4. Active: He repaired the car.

Passive: The car ___ by him.

5. Active: They built a house.

Passive: A house ___ by them.

6. Active: They cleaned the rooms.

Passive: The rooms ___ by them.

3. Future Simple (will)

7. Active: They will finish the work.

Passive: The work ___ by them.

8. Active: She will write a book.

Passive: A book ___ by her.

9. Active: They will build a bridge.

Passive: A bridge ___ by them.
